Chicken with Gongbao Sauce. In Cubes. Cool.

Trust me, bro.

Ingredients

Must Haves

  • Gochujang
  • Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Anything, really.

Nice To Haves

  • Anything, really.
  • I personally like the usual garnish ingredients though - green onions, korean chilli flakes, etc.

Steps

  1. Chop anything in any size.
  2. Stir fry and or boil.
  3. Add as much Gochujang as you like.
  4. Make sure to balance the ratio with sugar and soy sauce. Experiment until you get a feel for it. For starters, go for a 1:1:1 ratio to see how that tastes. Adjust later.
  5. Admire your hellish spicy sweet savory red korean creation and let the salvia build up in your mouth.
  6. When your salvia begins to drop onto the floor, begin to eat. Enjoy.

    7. side note: be careful with the red sauce, it can ruin everything that has any color but red
