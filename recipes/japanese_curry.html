Japanese Curry

It is instant but made with fresh ingredients except for the curry cubes. You will see what I mean.

Ingredients

Must Haves

  • Japanese curry cubes. You can get them in any asian supermarket.
  • At least potatos or anything you can bite on. Even noodles are fine.

Nice To Haves

  1. Meat
  2. More veggies, especially onions and carrots
  3. Soy sauce for extra flavor
  4. Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Cut all ingredients into cubes or small pieces.
  2. Stir fry for around 5 minutes.
  3. Add water and let it boil until all is soft
  4. Add curry cubes depending on how much flavor you would like the curry to have. You can even add soy sauce to add more flavor.
  5. Gently mix and let the curry cubes melt until everything looks like delicious diarrhea.
  6. Serve with rice or noodles.
  7. Enjoy ~
