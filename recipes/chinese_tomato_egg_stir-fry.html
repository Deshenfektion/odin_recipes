Chinese Tomato Egg Stir-Fry

Probably one of the most famous chinese dishes ever. Simply delicious. Again, only two ingredients needed: eggs and tomatos. Be prepared to cook like Chinese.

Ingredients

Must Haves

  • Eggs
  • Tomatos
  • Soy Sauce

Nice To Haves

  • Green onions
  • MSG
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Chop tomatos
  2. Make scrambled eggs and put aside
  3. Toss in the tomatos, stir fry until soft. Add water if necessary.
  4. Add the eggs.
  5. Mix well, add soy sauce and other sauces if you like.
  6. Garnish with green onions and serve!
